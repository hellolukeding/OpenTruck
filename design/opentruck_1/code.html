Pricing FAQ

How are tokens calculated?

Tokens are calculated based on both input (prompt) and output (completion). The total sum determines the credit deduction from your balance at the end of the request lifecycle.

What happens if I hit my rate limit?

On the Developer tier, requests exceeding the limit will receive a 429 status code. Pro and Enterprise tiers feature burst capacity and automatic queuing to ensure successful throughput.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades are applied at the end of your current billing cycle. Prorated credits are automatically issued to your decentralized wallet.
